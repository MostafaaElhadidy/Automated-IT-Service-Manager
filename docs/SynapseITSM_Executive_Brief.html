Executive Brief

SynapseITSM

An AI-powered IT support platform that diagnoses and resolves technical issues automatically — while keeping humans in control of every fix.
Prepared for Business Stakeholders  •  Confidential

▣What It Is

SynapseITSM lets employees describe IT problems in plain language and have them automatically triaged, diagnosed, and fixed — with a mandatory human approval checkpoint before any change is applied. It replaces slow, manual help-desk ticket handling with an intelligent system that investigates root cause and remediates issues end-to-end, including directly on a user's PC or laptop.

▣How It Works

  1. An employee reports a problem through a simple chat window (e.g. "the sales dashboard is down").
  2. The AI instantly checks whether it has seen this issue before and can answer immediately.
  3. If it's a real incident, the system reads live system-health data and maps service dependencies to pinpoint the true cause.
  4. It proposes a specific fix from a library of approved repair procedures ("runbooks").
  5. An IT operator reviews and approves the fix with one click — nothing runs without human sign-off.
  6. The fix is applied, verified, and the ticket closed automatically. Failed fixes are escalated with an email alert.

▣Key Differentiators

Human-in-the-Loop safety — Every corrective action requires explicit operator approval. The AI recommends; people decide. Critical for governance and risk control.
Remote remediation — Approved fixes can be applied directly to an employee's PC or laptop, even off-site, with no technical effort from the user.
Proactive monitoring — A background agent watches infrastructure 24/7 and opens high-priority tickets automatically the moment something breaks.
Self-improving knowledge base — Each resolved case feeds back into the system, continuously raising the automatic resolution rate.
Full audit trail — Every proposal, approval, and action is logged for compliance and reporting.

▣Business Value

  • Faster resolution — routine incidents handled in seconds instead of hours.
  • Lower support cost — fewer tickets reach human agents; staff are deployed where they matter.
  • Reduced downtime — early detection and automated fixes shorten outages.
  • Consistency & compliance — every action follows an approved procedure and is fully logged.
  • Scalability — absorbs growing ticket volume without proportional headcount growth.

▣Maturity & Technology

The platform is built on a modern, enterprise-grade foundation: multi-agent AI orchestration, secure role-based access, a containerized database, and a dedicated operations dashboard for the IT team. It supports flexible AI model providers, keeping it cost-effective and adaptable as the business scales.

SynapseITSM turns IT support from a reactive, manual cost center into a proactive, self-improving, mostly-automated service — while keeping humans firmly in control of every fix.
